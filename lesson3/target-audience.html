Target Audience

Middle-aged citizens of Paradise Island, more than likely small business owners in the community who wish to unit with other citizens and business owners.



Personas

#1: Todd -- Middle aged man running a small business with his family. Very involved in the community, but seeking to expand his network within the small business community in the Paradise Island area.

#2: Amanda -- Recent graduate from a 4 year university with a bachelors degree in Business Management.Just moved to Paradise Island, and seeking to meet other like-minded business owners through becoming a member of the Paradise Island Chamber of Commerce.



Scenarios

#1 Todd would like to find out about local events.
#2 Amanda would like to report severe weather in her area.
#3 Are there kid friendly events that Todd can attend with his children?
#4 Todd has a friend in a nearby city, will they be able to see the weather for Paradise Island?
#5 Amanda would like to unite with other business owners on Paradise Island.
