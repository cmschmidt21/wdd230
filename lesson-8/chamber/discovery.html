Brief History

Paradise Island is an Island in the Bahamas previously known as hog island. It is located just off the shore of Nassau which is located on the northern edge of the island of New Providence. Paradise Island is connected to New Providence by two bridges across the Naussau Harbor. Paradise Island was purchased in the 1980s for $79 million, then sold to Merv Griffin for $400 million. It was last sold for $125 million to the current owner, Sol Kerzner.
